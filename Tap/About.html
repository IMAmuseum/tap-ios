The National Museum of the American Indian-New York is located within the historic Alexander Hamilton U.S. Custom House. The museum's permanent and temporary exhibitions-as well as a range of public programs, including music and dance performances, films, and symposia-explore the diversity of the Native people of the Americas. Visit the museum website for more information about our current exhibitions.

HOURS & ADMISSION

The museum is open 10 AM-5 PM daily, Thursdays to 8 PM; closed December 25. Admission is free. Floor plans are available for download and printing.

Getting Here

The National Museum of the American Indian-New York, is located on the south side of Bowling Green, in lower Manhattan, adjacent to the northeast corner of Battery Park.

About the NMAI-NY app

The NMAI-NY app is designed to deliver multimedia tours of the museum's permanent and temporary exhibitions.

This app was created working with the Indianapolis Museum of Art using the free and open-source tools TourML & TAP. This project continues important work within the museum community to create mobile standards and open-source tools.
